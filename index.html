Disturbed - A Band Tribute Page

Bio

Disturbed is an American Heavy Metal Band originally formed in Chicago in 1994. Originally the band was named "Brawl" which changed in 1996 after David Draiman joined.

David Draiman's audition story is quite amusing. After auditioning for 20 other bands in the same month, he auditioned for "Brawl" and became an instant hit with his unique voice, great talent and a desire for creating original content. The other band members tried to hide their enthusiasm but truth was, they were ecsatic with him.
